Abstract

The Construction Tasks Ontology (CTO) describes tasks operating on construction elements, spatial zones and/or damages. The tasks are either planned or executed depending on the dataset metadata context of the dataset its used in. Five different types of tasks are defined: instalment, removal, modification, repair and inspection. Consequences of tasks on the dataset, i.e. added and/or deleted triples, are modeled using reified statements (RDF reification). The tasks can link to a reified statement using the CTO relations.

The namespace for CTO terms is https://w3id.org/cto#

The suggested prefix for the CTO ontology is cto

The Turtle version of the CTO ontology is available at https://w3id.org/cto/cto.ttl
The RDF/XML version of the CTO ontology is available at https://w3id.org/cto/cto.rdf
The JSON-LD version of the CTO ontology is available at https://w3id.org/cto/cto.jsonld
The N-Triples version of the CTO ontology is available at https://w3id.org/cto/cto.nt

The CTO ontology was created by Mathias Bonduel (KU Leuven) during his PhD research (2017-2020) funded by the Research Foundation Flanders in the form of a Strategic Basic Research grant (grant agreement 1S65917N).
